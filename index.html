Privacy Policy

This website only accesses public TikTok information to assist creators with compliance checks. No private data is stored or shared.

We only access public TikTok content, such as video scripts, captions, overlays, and audio for violation scanning purposes.

2. How We Use the Data

We use the data solely to provide TikTok Shop compliance guidance and to help creators fix potential violations.

3. No Storage or Resale

We do not store, share, sell, or retain any scanned data. All scans are temporary and automatically deleted after processing.
